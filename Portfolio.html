About Me

My name is Jeremy Lapidus, I am a journalism student at James Madison University. I grew up in Arlington, Virginia being a lifelong fan of sports.

I have a radio show on WXJM Harrisonburg, Virginia called the Triple Threat Show, where me and two of my friends talk sports for a hour and a half every Tuesday from 4:00 to 5:30 PM.

On top of that I also have an internship at a company called Relevnt where I write articles, host a shorter weekly show on my own, and join in on the main shows the company broadcasts.

You can contact me through email at jeremylapidus@yahoo.com or on the phone at 202-790-7951, thank you for stopping by and I hope you enjoy my stuff!
